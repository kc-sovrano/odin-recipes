Banana Soft Serve

Be the star of the show when you make this fan favorite in no time at all!

This recipe is perfect for all people, from the fitness guru looking for a low calorie snack to a busy mom who needs to give the kids a sweet treat! Using only 2 ingredients and, incredibly, only 1 step this delicious dessert will be winning the hearts of all that try it in virtually no time at all.

Ingredients

  • 6 bananas, peeled, chopped and frozen
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) lemon juice

Directions

  1. Process frozen banana and lemon juice in a food processor, scraping down sides frequently, until mixture is smooth and resembles soft serve ice-cream. Transfer to a metal loaf pan. Place in the freezer for 2 hours or until just firm but still scoop-able.
