Milk Truffles

There's nothing more satisfying than biting into luscious chocolate truffles,

especially when they're made with only two ingredients.

Have a sweet tooth that can't get enough chocolate? Need something that's not only delicious but takes 10 minutes or less to make? If you said yes to either of these, your in for a treat! These chocolate milk truffles come in batches of 16 and are guaranteed to impress. Don't eat too many!

Ingredients

  • 395g can NESTLÉ Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 30g (1/4 cup) NESTLÉ Plaistowe Premium Dutch Process Cocoa, plus extra to dust

Directions

  1. Place the milk and cocoa in a medium saucepan over low heat. Cook, stirring, for 8 minutes or until mixture is very thick. Transfer mixture to a bowl. Set aside to cool slightly before placing in the fridge for 1 hour to set.
  2. Use two teaspoonsful of the mixture to roll into smooth balls. Dust in extra cocoa powder to serve.
